The Assembly Line process

The guidance below is for organizations that want to set up their own copy of the Assembly Line, not for individual automators.

We think that the most important work we did came not from computer code, but a process: the process of automating a legal form, broken down into a series of small steps.

  3. AssemblyLine - Testing Template Board

Automating a form involves:

  • Research and vetting the substantive content
  • Coding
  • Adding labeled fields to a template document
  • Generating and refining a prototype
  • Testing and getting feedback
